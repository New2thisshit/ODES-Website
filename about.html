WHO WE ARE

Odirilejwang Enterprise (formerly ODES) was founded in 2010 with the vision of becoming the preferred provider of human resource solutions in South Africa. We offer HR consulting, SETA-compliant training, employee wellness and recruitment services.

Our approach is rooted in compliance, partnership and performance. We deliver impactful solutions that exceed expectations.
